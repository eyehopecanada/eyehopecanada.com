A special thanks to:

Our team had the privilege of interviewing 14 cancer survivors, patients, and their carers. We collaborated with a group of artists and writers to translate their experiences into creative pieces. Resilience: Stories of Cancer Survivorship is a colourful collection of paintings, digital art, short stories, and poems about the strength and resilience of individuals diagnosed with cancer. The experiences recounted in this book capture a small part of our interviewees' lives. We encourage you to follow our interviewees' social media accounts and support their awareness and fundraising initiatives.

Thank you so much to everyone who made this book possible! All profits collected from this book were donated to Childhood Cancer Canada.
